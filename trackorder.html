Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find my order number?

Your order number can be found in your order confirmation email or on your order history page.

What is an AWS number?

An AWS number (Air Waybill) is a tracking number provided by the shipping carrier.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on your location. Please refer to your order confirmation.

My order is delayed. What should I do?

If your order is delayed, contact customer support with your order and AWS number.

How do I return an item?

To return an item, visit our returns page and follow the instructions.
