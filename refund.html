Return & Refund Policy

Thank you for shopping at Furniture Store. We strive to provide the best products and services. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

1. Return Policy

You can return a product within 7 days of delivery if:

  • The product is defective or damaged upon arrival.
  • The wrong product was delivered.
  • The product is not as described on the website.

Note: The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and with all accessories included.

2. Refund Policy

Once we receive and inspect your returned product, we will notify you about the refund status:

  • If approved, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method.
  • If your return is rejected, we will notify you and return the item to you.

3. Non-Returnable Items

Certain items cannot be returned, including:

  • Customized or made-to-order furniture.
  • Items damaged due to misuse or improper handling.
  • Products without the original packaging.

4. How to Initiate a Return

To request a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Email us at support@furniturestore.com with your order details.
  2. Attach clear images of the product and issue.
  3. Our team will verify your request and arrange a pickup.

5. Exchange Policy

If you wish to exchange an item, contact us within 7 days of delivery. Exchanges are subject to stock availability.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our return and refund policy, reach out to us at support@furniturestore.com.
